Lasagna

A fancy pasta dish with wide strips of pasta.

It contains layers of meat and veggies with cheese and tomato sauce.

It's pretty good.

Inrgredients:

  • Lasagna
  • Mozzarella
  • egg
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Parmesan
  • Beef
  • Sauce

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & Bake
